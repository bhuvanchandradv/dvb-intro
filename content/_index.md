Senior Systems Engineer specialising in Mechatronics and Embedded Systems, based in Tokyo. I work at [Rapyuta Robotics](https://www.rapyuta-robotics.com/) on autonomous warehouse robotics — across the full embedded stack, from real-time firmware and motor control to system architecture and reliability engineering.

I'm on a trajectory toward Systems Architecture: thinking about how complex robotic systems fit together at scale, and increasingly interested in developer tooling and AI-assisted workflows that make embedded teams more effective.

Before Tokyo, I built my foundation at [Toradex](https://www.toradex.com/) working across industrial embedded platforms and communication protocols — and picked up upstream [Linux kernel](https://git.kernel.org/pub/scm/linux/kernel/git/torvalds/linux.git/log/?qt=author&q=bhuvanchandra.dv) and [U-Boot](http://git.denx.de/?p=u-boot.git&a=search&h=refs%2Fheads%2Fmaster&st=author&s=bhuvanchandra.dv) contributions along the way. That background in real hardware constraints — timing, memory, determinism — still shapes how I approach every problem.

I write about embedded systems, robotics, real-time OS internals, and the craft of building reliable systems in production.
